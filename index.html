Our Story

Established in 2005, Jimma Medical Drug Store has grown into a household name in the pharmaceutical retail industry in the Oromia region. Throughout the years, we have transitioned from a single neighborhood pharmacy to a 3-branch operation across Jimma.

Apart from the supply of essential pharmaceutical products, we also provide medical equipment, cosmetics, baby products, compounding, and bulk wholesale services to clinics and hospitals. We are deeply embedded in the community, providing a line of credit and reliable supply chains to institutional partners.

Our Purpose

To provide the highest quality products and services that enhance the well-being of humanity. We strive to constantly improve and innovate to meet the evolving needs of our customers and make a lasting impact on the healthcare landscape in Ethiopia.

Our Vision

To enhance accessibility to the finest pharmaceutical products, services, and medical equipment, effectively bridging the gap between supply and demand. We are driven to ensure all individuals and institutions have reliable access to healthcare essentials.

Our Mission

To instill hope in the public by delivering exceptional quality healthcare solutions through the retailing of pharmaceutical goods and services. We aim to empower individuals to take charge of their well-being through commitment to excellence and innovation.
